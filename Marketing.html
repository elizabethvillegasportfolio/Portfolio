Marketing

Persona Research

Guilt-Free Bakery

For my content and strategy project, I created a customer persona that would be the ideal fit as a client for our gluten-free bakery. This persona helped to define the characteristics and preferences of the target audience most likely to engage with our products.

XMPIE Chart

Guilt-Free Project

For my project, I created an XMPIE chart for my gluten-free bakery to analyze and present important data related to customer demographics, purchasing patterns, and product preferences. The chart helped to visualize key insights, enabling us to better understand our audience and tailor our marketing strategies accordingly.

E-Tailing

Mock Application

For my e-tailing project, I developed an application designed to help individuals with gluten allergies easily find gluten-free dining options. The app provides a quick and user-friendly way for users to search for nearby restaurants, cafes, and stores offering gluten-free choices, making it easier for them to make informed decisions and enjoy a safe dining experience.

Application Research

Room Home Planner

For my Augmented Reality project, I conducted research on an application that leverages augmented reality (AR) to enhance the real estate industry. This app would allow potential buyers and renters to visualize properties in immersive 3D, view virtual tours, and even make design modifications in real-time.
