Branding

logo

VCI Group Logo

I had the opportunity to design a refreshed logo for a framing business in Houston, Texas, seeking a brand update. The objective was to modernize the company’s visual identity while preserving its core values and strong ties to the local community. I focused on creating a logo that encapsulated the business's craftsmanship and attention to detail, ensuring it would also appeal to a wider audience. By incorporating contemporary design elements, the new logo helps the company stand out in a competitive market. The final result not only enhances the brand’s presence but also provides a fresh, professional image that better resonates with potential customers.

LinkedIn Banner

VCI Group LinkedIn Banner

I designed a LinkedIn banner for the framing company to highlight some of their best work while seamlessly integrating the newly refreshed logo. The banner was created with the intention of showcasing the company’s craftsmanship and attention to detail, offering a visual representation of their expertise in the framing industry. By incorporating the logo, I ensured brand consistency and strengthened the company’s visual identity across its social media presence. The design not only emphasizes the quality of their services but also presents a professional and polished image to potential clients, contributing to a stronger and more cohesive online brand presence.

Business Cards

Huatan Internship

For the newly acquired garden, I designed business cards to facilitate effective networking among attendees.
